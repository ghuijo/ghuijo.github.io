Hello! I am an AI researcher working on designing deep neural networks for computer vision...

I am currently affiliated at B2X, an AI technology company in Seoul, Korea.
Before joining B2X, I completed my M.S. in Artificial Intelligence Convergence advised by Prof. Jang-Hwan Choi and B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering at Ewha Womans University (Aug. 2014 and Aug. 2022).

I am highly interested in applying AI for various Vision, 3-dimensional, Human Pose, and Medical fields. My research topics have been focused on Computer Vision, Facial recognition, and generative models. I also have research experiences in Human Pose Recognition, Medical Imaging, and Protein-Protein Interaction Prediction.
